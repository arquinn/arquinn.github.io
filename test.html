About

Buzzsaw \ˈbəz ˈsȯ\: A microbrewery striving for perfection through craft. Born from the minds of three college friends and teammates who wanted to make something simple, bold and different. It offers a line of beers that are both unique and familiar. Craftsmanship ensures every beer is true to the nature of its pure ingredients, while still surfacing new profiles and combinations that are pleasantly out of the ordinary.

Buzzsaw Brew Co.: Enjoy the Craft.

We at Buzzsaw Brew Co utilize both traditional and innovative methods to turn nature's best ingredients into some of the greatest beers you will ever have. Our brews are the fruit of a labor of love, of countless hours of research, and of the experiences we've had who make us who we are. Buzzsaw Brew Co teases out all of our senses, taking us back to simpler times while creating new experiences to shape what we all are. The Buzzsaw Tap Room will be a place where drinking a beer with your friends is more than just a pastime—it is an experience to be cherished and shared. We are a community-oriented, and as such we seek to build relationships giving back to the people who help us do what we love. We believe in making the world a better place through making good beer, and our brewing team has wholeheartedly embraced this never-ending journey for the perfect brew. We hope you join us along the way.

Beers

  • The Buzzsaw Double IPA
  • Pale, rye and light caramel malts are very well balanced against the warrior, amarillo, simcoe and cascade hops. There are a lot of hops. 9.5% ABV 104 IBU
  • White Dot Belgian Wit
  • A spicy Belgian-style ale brewed with wheat and coriander. Very refreshing and light. 5% ABV 20 IBU
  • Lagerhythm Vienna Lager
  • Rust colored, sweet and brewed with Germany tradition in mind. Lagering for one month makes this beer exceptionally smooth and balanced. The caramel-forward flavor, vienna malt and noble hops are delicious. Definitely Gemütlichkeit. 4.7% ABV 19 IBU
  • The Hill Pale Ale
  • We used vienna as the base malt. Wholesome hoppage with Mosaic gives us a wonderful array of aromas. 4.7% 45 IBU
  • Singularity Imperial Stout
  • True to its namesake, it is the darkest beer we will ever brew. Roasted malts, maple syrup and brown sugar contrasted with a subtle bitterness, makes Singularity a massive beer, relatively speaking... 9.5% ABV 55 IBU
  • Secret Stash Belgian Tripel
  • Pilsner malt, wheat and lots of sugar makes Secret Stash a bit sweet, a bit dry and very tasty. Brewed traditionally with Saaz and Tettnang hops and a pinch of coriander for good measure. 9.2% ABV 27 IBU
  • Booth’s Blood Strong English Ale
  • The rainbow of malts in this beer makes it a mouthful. Barley, corn, oats and molasses impart a dark, bready, sweetness. Warm and cheerful, this one will cure whatever ails you. 10% ABV 58 IBU

Investors

  • Can I invest in BuzzSaw Brew Company?
  • Yes. BuzzSaw Brew Co is taking investments from anyone interested in owning a part of our passion. With minimum investments starting at $5,000.00, BuzzSaw Brew Co is selling community oriented equity in hopes of raising $700,000.00.
  • What do I get with an investment in BuzzSaw Brew Company?
  • Your investment will yield you equity in proportion to your initial contribution and the total cost of $700,000.00.
  • Do the founders of BuzzSaw Brew Company have equity?
  • The founders will not start with any equity, and will rather earn equity as your investment is paid back. Once you receive your initial investment back, the founders will gain 25% equity. Once you receive two-times your initial investment, the founders will gain 40% equity. Once you receive three-times your initial investment, the founders will gain 60% equity—the maximum reserved for them.
  • What is the projected payback period for my investment?
  • We project your initial investment to be returned by the end of Year 3, for it to double by the end of year 5, and for it to triple by year 7.
  • When will BuzzSaw Brew Company be open?
  • We will be closing financing December 31, 2016, with the target of opening our doors in the Summer of 2017.
