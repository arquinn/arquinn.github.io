A Bit of Background

I first became interested in Computer Science during an introductory class in high school, and have been passionate about CS ever since. I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Math from Denison University in Graville, Ohio. After graduation I spent a 15 months working for IBM. While I found the work at IBM interesting, I ultimately decided that I wanted to do research in operating systems.

I started pursuing a Ph.D. at the University of Michigan in the fall of 2015. Since then I've been working on understanding how we can leverage distributed systems to better understand how information flows through applications.
